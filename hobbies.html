Hobbies

Movies and TV Series

At home, I enjoy watching movies and TV series for both pleasure and to improve my vocabulary. My favorite genres include Sci-Fi, action, comedy, and some thrillers. However, I also like to go to the cinema to watch new and classic movies. Recently, I have watched movies and TV shows such as The Lord of the Rings, The Hobbit, Fast and Furious, most of the Marvel movies, The Last of Us, Friends, and Modern Family.

Eat and drink

Normally, I enjoy cooking at home, but occasionally, I like to dine out and try something different. My absolute favorite cuisine is Italian, particularly dishes like pasta, lasagna, and pizza – they are my weakness. Being Brazilian, I may be biased, but I can't resist our traditional food and desserts either! Speaking of beverages, I prefer beer or a glass of wine over any other drink. There's nothing quite like having a cold beer and delicious Brazilian barbecue by the pool on a hot day!

Travel

I believe that spending money on food and travel is a fascinating thing to do. Exploring new places, countries, and cultures can be enriching for our souls. Often, I find myself wondering if I should move to a particular place after experiencing it. Traveling allows us to become better versions of ourselves by exposing us to all that the world has to offer.
